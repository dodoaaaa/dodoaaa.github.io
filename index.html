I am a second-year M.S. student in the Robotics and Automation Lab at Jeonbuk National University, Jeonju, South Korea. I am expected to graduate in June 2026 and am actively seeking PhD positions for 2026 Fall semester.

I am currently developing quadruped manipulators capable of precise and robust operation in unstructured environments. My research addresses hardware limitations such as structural compliance and software challenges including multi-task execution and long-horizon trajectory optimization. I leverage reinforcement learning to improve locomotion and manipulation performance, laying the foundation for extending these methods to aerial and humanoid mobile manipulators.

I'm interested in industrial-level mobile manipulators and multi-modal locomotion for all-terrain mobility!
